Axolotl's Diet

Home | Diet | Anatomy

Axolotls can’t chew their food, so they use a suction technique to swallow their food whole. Some of the foods axolotls eat are:

In the wild

  • Small insects
  • Fish
  • Larvae
  • Worms
  • Mollusks

Recommended foods in captivity

  • Nightcrawlers
  • Blackworms
  • Daphina
  • Blood worms
  • Ghost shrimps
  • Brine shrimps
  • Pellets

Axolotls also eat their sibling’s limbs, since it’s sometimes hard to find food in their habitat. The axolotl will just regrow the limb in a couple weeks, so no harm done!
